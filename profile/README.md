**AI-native market intelligence infrastructure for Indian equities.**

Manasija Technologies builds products that help investors, traders, fintech teams, and AI agents understand market-moving information faster. We combine market data, corporate filings, announcements, earnings, concalls, news, alerts, and LLM-native workflows into practical tools for Indian equity markets.

## What we build

### Myuki Go

A mobile-first market monitoring app for retail investors.

Myuki Go helps users track watchlists, understand why stocks move, summarize filings and announcements, and receive relevant market alerts in plain English.

### Myuki for Traders

An audio-first desktop assistant for active traders and market professionals.

Myuki for Traders monitors market events such as filings, results, block deals, order wins, QIPs, unusual volume, and price moves, then delivers timely voice alerts and trading briefs.

### Drishti APIs

Developer APIs for Indian equities intelligence.

Drishti provides structured APIs, WebSocket streams, batch workflows, and agent-ready market intelligence for fintech apps, research platforms, trading assistants, portfolio monitors, and enterprise workflows.

### Market Stack

Self-hosted market workflow infrastructure.

Market Stack provides broker-aware trading workspace components, portfolio data workflows, quote infrastructure, alerting, and market intelligence tooling for developers and trading teams.
